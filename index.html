Dept. of Art+Design

The South Carolina School of the Arts Department of Art + Design is proud to announce the first Student Juried Exhibition and Competition. This exhibition highlights a selection of outstanding student work submitted into the categories of Foundations, Graphic Design, Photography, Painting & Drawing, Digital/Video, 3D/Ceramics, Printmaking, and Mixed-Media.

150 pieces were submitted and 48 entries were selected to be shown in the final exhibit. Out of the chosen entries a select few have been awarded Best in Show and Judge's Choice.

Debbie Millman
Graphic Design Juror

Debbie Millman



Debbie Millman is a designer, author, curator and educator. Since 2005, she has been the host of the award-winning podcast Design Matters, which is one of the world’s first and longest running podcasts. She is also co-founder and chair of the world’s first graduate program in branding at the School of Visual Arts, editorial director of Print magazine, and the author of six books on design and branding. She has worked on the design and strategy of over 200 of the world’s biggest brands and is currently Chair of the Board of Directors for Law & Order SVU actor and activist Mariska Hargitay’s Joyful Heart Foundation.

Jessica Burke
Fine Art Juror

Jessica Burke



Jessica Burke, JB, is a figurative artist and educator that has been teaching at the university level for 14+ years. She is an Associate Professor of Art and Coordinator of the Foundations Program at the University of North Carolina in Charlotte. She has always been fascinated with mass media and interested in exploring various interpretations on the impact of popular culture on identity and gender. Her creative work has been included in competitive group and solo exhibitions nationally and internationally including the Koehnline Museum of Art in Illinois; the Élysees Modern and Contemporary Art Fair in Paris, France; the Toshima Gallery in Tokyo, Japan; the LuXun Academy of Fine Arts, in Shenyang, China and the Kepco Plaza Gallery Museum, Seoul, South Korea. Her work is part of private, state and corporate collections that include the City of North Charleston; the City of Savannah, Georgia; Seminole State College, Florida and the National Living Treasure Museum in Yugawara, Japan.

The South Carolina School of the Arts Department of Art+Design is a premiere southeastern art and design program housed inside of a broad liberal arts education. As a part of Anderson University, we offer a BFA in Graphic Design and a BA in Art with concentrations in Painting & Drawing, Sculpture & Ceramics, Photography, and Art Education. Small class sizes and low faculty to student ratio are what makes us one of the Best Regional Universities according to U.S. News and World Report.

This is the first in what is planned to be an annual Art+Design Student Juried Exhibition and Competition. Our students do the real work of art and it shows. The program is rigorous. The environment is intense. Expectations are high. The reward for all that effort? Solution-focused, award-winning, career-launching work.
