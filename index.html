The South Carolina School of the Arts Department of Art + Design is proud to announce the selections of the first annual Student Juried Exhibition and Competition. This exhibition highlights a selection of outstanding student work submitted into the categories of Foundations, Graphic Design, Photography, Painting Drawing, Digital/Video, 3D/Ceramics, Printmaking, and Mixed-Media.

Two jurors judged over 120 submissions and selected 47 to be a part of the exhibit. Selected fine art and graphic design have been awarded Best in Show and Judge's choice awards respectively. Congratulations to all of those that have been selected!

The South Carolina School of the Arts Department of Art+Design is a premiere southeastern art and design program housed inside of a broad liberal arts education. As a part of Anderson University, we offer a BFA in Graphic Design and a BA in Art with concentrations in Painting & Drawing, Sculpture & Ceramics, Photography, and Art Education. Small class sizes and low faculty to student ratio are what makes us one of the Best Regional Universities according to U.S. News and World Report.

This is the first in what is planned to be an annual Art+Design Juried Exhibition and Competition. Our students do the real work of art and it shows. The program is rigorous. The environment is intense. Expectations are high. The reward for all that effort? Solution-focused, award-winning, career-launching work.
