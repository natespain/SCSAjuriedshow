Dept. of Art+Design

The South Carolina School of the Arts Department of Art + Design is proud to announce the selections of the first annual Student Juried Exhibition and Competition. This exhibition highlights a selection of outstanding student work submitted into the categories of Foundations, Graphic Design, Photography, Painting Drawing, Digital/Video, 3D/Ceramics, Printmaking, and Mixed-Media.

Two jurors judged over 120 submissions and selected 47 to be a part of the exhibit. Selected fine art and graphic design have been awarded Best in Show and Judge's choice awards respectively. Congratulations to all of those that have been selected!

Christ-centered, concept-driven, future-focused.

AU students and alumni have been featured in prestigious publications like Print Magazine and Communication Arts, exhibited in the Museum of Design Atlanta (MODA), and won accolades from the American Institute of Graphic Arts (AIGA) InState Awards and over 250 American Advertising Federation Awards.

Alumni work for a variety of national companies, including Yelp! in San Francisco, Draft Kings in Boston, and Sotheby’s in New York City. AU grads are not just designers, but Art Directors at top creative agencies. Our students have interned under industry renowned creatives, such as Debbie Millman and James Victore. We host innovative thinkers like Elle Luna and James Sherraden of Hatch Show Print.

Graphic Design at Anderson University. Creativity, with teeth.
